in Computer Engineering

Rationale

Based on the Guidelines for the Implemenation of CMO 46 series of 2012 and CMO 37 s 2012, the PSG implements shift to outcomes based education leading to competency based standards. It specifies the "core competencies" expected of BS Computer Engineering graduates "regardless of the type of Higher Education Institutions (HEI)" they graduate from."

However, in recognition of outcomes-based education (OBE) and the typology of HEIs, the PSG also provide ample space for HEIs to innovate in the curriculum in line with the assessment of how best to achieve learning outcomes in their particular contexts and their respective missions.

Program Description

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) is a program that embodies the science and technology of design, development, implementation, maintenance and integration of software and hardware components in modern computer systems and computer controlled equipment.

SPECIALIZATION

Artificial Intelligence

Rationale

Artificial Intelligence (AI) will change the way how we lived and work as individuals and a society. AI will fundamentally transform business processes and the end-user experience on a continuous basis by removing not only time and distance factors but also human limitations.

It will bring machine learning together with deep learning (hidden data) to society to drive smarter systems and innovation.

Rationale (cont'd)

AI can be used to build predictive models, learn how software can be used to process, analyze, and exteract meaning from natural language; and to process images and video to understand the world the way we do.

The potential of AI extends far beyond helping organizational processes run faster or more efficiently, it will revolutionized them.
